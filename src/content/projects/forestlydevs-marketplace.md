## Overview

ForestlyDevs began as a place by developers, for developers, with the motto *Grow Together*. The platform hosted portfolios, job postings, and project listings for Roblox and game-development creators.

## Operating work

- Ran the two-sided marketplace end to end
- Onboarded and coached 100+ developers, converting interest into 40 new projects in one month
- Redesigned onboarding flows based on drop-off analysis — 500%+ engagement lift the following week
- Connected users with 8,500+ investment opportunities, portfolios, and job postings

## Relevance

Shows ability to create and operate a platform business — not just write code, but grow usage and facilitate real collaborations.
